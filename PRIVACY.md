# Privacy Policy — Tradeelo POD Publisher

Last updated: 2026-05-12

This application ("Tradeelo POD Publisher") is used solely for automated pin publishing to the Tradeelo Pinterest business account.

## Data Collection
None. This app does not collect, store, or share any personal data from users or viewers.

## Data Usage
Pin images and descriptions are generated from product mockups owned by Tradeelo. The app posts these pins to designated Pinterest boards for marketing purposes only.

## Third-Party Services
- Pinterest API: Used exclusively for posting pins to the Tradeelo business account.
- GitHub Pages: Hosts this privacy policy document only.
